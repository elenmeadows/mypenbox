hi there,

welcome to my pen-box, the site where you can play around with pencils, markers and other artist supplies.

— Hey! What does «play around» mean?
— Well, let me explain. Y'know, life is so much easier when you have all of your things up to date, in one place. And *surprise!* THIS IS WHAT MY PEN-BOX CAN HELP YOU WITH!

Sign up and you'll have access to the library of stuff used for drawing. Then manage it! Create your own sets of supplies, add them to wishlist and favourites, write notes, check their condition (maybe it's time to buy another one).. Sounds great, isn't it? ;)
